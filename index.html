📚 Conversion Formulas:

Celsius to Fahrenheit

°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32

Celsius to Kelvin

K = °C + 273.15

Fahrenheit to Celsius

°C = (°F - 32) × 5/9

Fahrenheit to Kelvin

K = (°F - 32) × 5/9 + 273.15

Kelvin to Celsius

°C = K - 273.15

Kelvin to Fahrenheit

°F = (K - 273.15) × 9/5 + 32

🌡️ Quick Reference Points:

Water Freezes:

0°C = 32°F = 273.15K

Room Temperature:

20°C = 68°F = 293.15K

Body Temperature:

37°C = 98.6°F = 310.15K

Water Boils:

100°C = 212°F = 373.15K
